About Me

I genuinely love what I do. I'm passionate about using technology to enhance and engage customers. I love meeting new clients and engaging in meaningful, successful partnerships that result in amazing user experiences.

I'm a Leader

I've always found myself at the head of a team or teams, and it's because I am driven to be on the best performing team, producing the best results. I know how to work with, look after, and get the best out of people.

I'm a Technologist

I love problem solving. As a technologist I know how to go about solving a complex problem by connecting the right people, technology, and business opportunities. I am very hands on; I enjoy getting stuck in and both architecting and delivering solutions.

Why I do it

I do it because the challenges keep coming, the technology keeps changing, and that keeps me interested. I like being able to design and build something smart that's scalable, performant, and delights users.
